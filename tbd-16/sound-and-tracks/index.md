# Sound & Tracks

Every track in the **tbd 16** has its own sound engine — a *Machine* — its own parameters, and its own mixer channel. This section covers everything that shapes **what a track sounds like**. For a full catalog of sound engines, see the dedicated [Machines]({{ site.baseurl }}/tbd-16/machines/) section.
{: .fs-6 .fw-300 }

---

## How it's organised

- **Sound Parameters** are the synthesis/sample controls — what kind of sound it is.
- **Track Parameters** are per-track performance controls like pitch, length, arpeggiator, and euclidean generator.
- **Track Mixer** is the per-track mix — level, pan, and effect sends.
- **Macros & Presets** explain how a Machine becomes a playable instrument, and how to browse and save sounds.
