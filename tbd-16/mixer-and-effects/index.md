# Mixer & Effects

Once your tracks sound right on their own, the Mixer & Effects section is where you balance them, colour them, and shape the master output.
{: .fs-6 .fw-300 }

---

## Signal flow at a glance

Each track → Track Mixer (level, pan, FX sends) → Global Mixer → FX busses & Master → Outputs.

There are two global send effects (Delay and Reverb), a global mixer for balancing all 16 tracks at once, and a master section with limiter and output gain.
