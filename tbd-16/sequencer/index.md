# Sequencer

The sequencer is the heart of the **tbd 16** — a 16-track step sequencer where each track can play its own pattern at its own length.
{: .fs-6 .fw-300 }

---

## How it works

Each of the 16 tracks has **6 pattern slots**, each up to **64 steps** long. Tracks can play different patterns simultaneously, so you can build evolving, polyrhythmic grooves.

Steps aren't just on/off — every step can hold **parameter locks** that override sound or mixer parameters for that step only, giving you dynamic, expressive sequences without touching the knobs.

Tempo is set globally, and the **tbd 16** can sync to external MIDI clock or Ableton Link.
