# Hardware

Physical layout, connections, controls, and specifications of the **tbd 16**.
{: .fs-6 .fw-300 }

---

## At a glance

The **tbd 16** is a compact desktop instrument. All controls sit on the **top**, all audio and sync jacks on the **right side**, power and computer connections on the **left side**, and the SD card slot on the **bottom**.

| Where to find it | Section |

## Top — controls

![tbd 16 — top view](../../../images/tbd-16/dadamachines-tbd16-drawing.png)

| Area | Controls |
|:-----|:---------|
| Upper left / right | **FUNC1** (Project) and **FUNC2** (Global) with status LEDs |
| Below the display | 4 rotary push-encoders (**KNOB 1–4**) |
| Center left | D-Pad (**↑ ↓ ← →**) |
| Center | **REC**, **PLAY**, **SHIFT**, **HYPER** |
| Center right | **FUNC3–FUNC6** (Sound, Pattern, A, B) |
| Bottom rows | 16 RGB-backlit step buttons |
{: .dada-minimal-table }

A 2.4″ OLED display sits in the upper centre. A dedicated volume wheel sits on the right side of the top panel.

---

## Right side — audio and MIDI

*(placeholder image — right side of the device)*

- Stereo line in (TRS 3.5 mm)
- Stereo line out (TRS 3.5 mm)
- Headphone out (TRS 3.5 mm)
- TRS MIDI 1 In / Out
- TRS MIDI 2 In / Out (TRS MIDI 2 In doubles as Eurorack Clock/Reset input)

---

## Left side — power and computer

*(placeholder image — left side of the device)*

- **USB-C** — power and connection to a computer (MIDI, web interface over USB network)
- **USB-A** — host port for class-compliant USB MIDI controllers (Launchpad, Launchkey, etc.)
- Secondary USB-C ports for additional power / development use

---

## Bottom — SD card and MIDI/Clock switch

*(placeholder image — bottom of the device)*

- **SD card slot** — all project data, presets, and samples are stored here
- **MIDI 2 / Clock switch** — toggles TRS MIDI 2 In between standard MIDI and analog Clock/Reset for Eurorack sync

---

## LEDs

The **tbd 16** has 21 RGB LEDs that provide visual feedback:

| LEDs | Position | Purpose |
|:-----|:---------|:--------|
| 1–16 | Under the step buttons | Step state, playback position, track activity |
| 17–18 | Above FUNC1 / FUNC2 | Configurable status indicators |
| 19–21 | Internal | System status |
{: .dada-minimal-table }

Brightness and colour theme can be adjusted in [Settings → Display](../project-and-settings/display-settings).
