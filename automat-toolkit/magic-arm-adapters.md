{: .fs-9 }

## What it is

The **Magic Arm Adapter** is a 3D-printed part that lets you attach a dadamachines solenoid beater to any standard Magic Arm. Ideal for experimenting with unconventional placements — above a found object, around a sculpture, clamped to a table edge — anywhere you go searching for sounds.

---

## Openness

As always, dadamachines embraces user creativity. The STL design is available for **free download**, so you can print the adapter yourself, remix it for your own beaters, or scale it to fit a specific setup.

---

## What's in the set

- **6 Magic Arm Adapters** — ready-made, ready to clip on.
- Compatible with standard 1/4"-20 Magic Arm clamps.

---

## Availability

A set of 6 Magic Arm Adapters is **19 € + VAT**, or print your own from the downloadable STL.
