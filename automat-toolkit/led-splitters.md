## What it is

The **LED Splitter** is a small inline adapter that sits between an automat output and a solenoid beater. It passes the trigger signal through to the solenoid as normal, and lights an LED on every hit. For the first time, audiences can see both the physical movement and a clear visual signal for each trigger.

---

## What's in the set

- **12 LED Splitters** — one per output on an automat controller.
- Fully passive — plug between output and beater, no additional power required.
- Colour: white LED (matches the dadamachines aesthetic).
